# FridaMusic TM Open Source Policy

## Licensing baseline
- Source code is licensed under Apache License 2.0.
- Contributions, reuse, and forks are welcome under Apache 2.0 terms.

## Brand and identity separation
Open-source code licensing does not transfer ownership of:
- FridaMusic TM or Frida Labs TM branding
- Official artwork and visual identity
- Official distribution identity and listings

Contributing to the repository does not grant trademark ownership or control over official brand assets.

## Collaboration philosophy
FridaMusic TM follows a pragmatic open-source approach:
- Keep collaboration accessible
- Avoid unnecessary contribution barriers
- Preserve clear legal boundaries for brand protection
- Maintain a scalable governance model for future growth

## Growth and ecosystem readiness
This policy is designed for startup scalability, allowing:
- Community-driven development
- Professional release governance
- Expansion to additional Frida Labs TM products
