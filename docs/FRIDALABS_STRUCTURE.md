# Frida Labs TM Ecosystem Structure

## Organizational map

```text
Frida Labs TM
│
├── FridaMusic TM
│   ├── Android App
│   ├── Web Platform
│   ├── Metadata Services
│   ├── Release Infrastructure
│   └── Design System
│
├── Future Projects
│   ├── Experimental Services
│   ├── APIs
│   ├── AI Systems
│   └── Future Platforms
```

## Governance model
- Frida Labs TM acts as the parent organization and strategic umbrella.
- FridaMusic TM is a product division inside the Frida Labs TM ecosystem.
- The architecture is designed to support multi-product expansion over time.

## Scalability intent
This structure enables:
- Shared legal and branding governance
- Reusable technical infrastructure
- Future service and platform diversification
- Professional startup-grade operating model
