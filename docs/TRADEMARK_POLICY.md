Even if a formal trademark registration is pending or not yet completed, these identifiers remain protected as project identity assets.

## Nominative use (allowed)
You may use the names in a descriptive manner, such as:
- "Fork of FridaMusic TM"

Nominative use must not imply sponsorship, endorsement, or official affiliation.

## Restricted commercial or public-distribution use
Without explicit written authorization, you may not:
- Market or distribute derivatives under FridaMusic TM or Frida Labs TM as if official.
- Use confusingly similar product branding for stores, websites, or binaries.
- Repackage and redistribute builds in a misleading way.

## Anti-impersonation rule
Impersonation of official channels, releases, social profiles, or store listings is prohibited.

## Relationship with Apache 2.0
Apache 2.0 governs source code rights. Trademark and branding rights remain separate and are not automatically licensed to contributors or fork maintainers.
